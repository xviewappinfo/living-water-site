Living Water International Ministry was born out of a vision to create a vibrant, English-speaking worship community within the Chinese Christian Church of Virginia at Springfield (CCCVA-SP).

Recognizing the growing number of English-speaking members — teens, young adults, and families — our church launched this ministry to provide a space where everyone can worship, learn, and grow in their own heart language while remaining connected to the broader CCCVA-SP family.

The name "Living Water" comes from Jesus' promise in John 7:38 — that whoever believes in Him will have rivers of living water flowing from within. We believe this promise is for every generation and every culture, and we seek to be a community where that living water flows freely.
