## Sermon Notes

Many of us grow up navigating between cultures, expectations, and identities. But Scripture tells us our deepest identity is found in who God says we are.

### Key Points

1. Who we were — dead in trespasses and sins
2. What God did — made us alive together with Christ
3. Who we are — His workmanship, created for good works
4. How we live — walking in the identity He has given us
