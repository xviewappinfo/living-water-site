## Sermon Notes

In this message, we explore Jesus' radical invitation at the Feast of Tabernacles. What did it mean for His original audience, and what does it mean for us today?

### Key Points

1. **The Setting** — Understanding the Feast of Tabernacles and why Jesus' words were so powerful
2. **The Invitation** — "If anyone is thirsty, let him come to me and drink"
3. **The Promise** — Rivers of living water flowing from within
4. **The Application** — How we receive and share this living water in our daily lives
